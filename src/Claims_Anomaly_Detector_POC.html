Flagged claims — reason codes

Each flag shows the deviation that triggered it — this is the payload Node 2 (the Agentic Claims Investigator) receives for evidence-backed investigation
Method: for each provider's claim sequence, a rolling mean and standard deviation are computed over a trailing window. Any claim whose billed amount deviates from that rolling mean by more than the selected z-score threshold is flagged, with the reason code reporting the exact deviation. This is Node 1 of the Cotiviti Agentic Audit Copilot — it decides which claims are statistically worth investigating. Flagged claims are designed to hand off to Node 2, a generative-AI reasoning layer that investigates why and produces an auditable recommendation for a human analyst. Illustrative only, not a production fraud model.
